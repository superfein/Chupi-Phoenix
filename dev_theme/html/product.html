Details

Our signature Queen of Hearts is set with a moissanite gemstone surrounded by a crown classic diamonds on a solid 14 carat gold band.

Moissanite is the most brilliantly sparkling gemstone, grown in a lab with minimal environmental impact. We have sourced our moissanite gemstones from New York bringing you a hand cut, socially responsible and eternally brilliant gemstone. The perfect modern gemstone for your modern heirloom.

Size & Materials

  • Casts in solid 14k gold
  • 5.5mm round moissanite
  • Halo of 1.3mm Classic Diamonds
  • 14k Solid Gold Twig Band
